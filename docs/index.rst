How to Perform a NordPass Account Login
---------------------------------------

Follow these steps to sign in to your NordPass account:

1. Open the NordPass application, browser extension, or official login page.
2. Enter the email address connected to your account.
3. Enter your Master Password.
4. Complete any required security verification.
5. Access your encrypted password vault.

Common NordPass Account Login Issues
------------------------------------

Incorrect Email Address or Master Password
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Incorrect login information is one of the most common reasons users cannot access their accounts.

Check the following:

* Confirm your registered email address.
* Enter your Master Password carefully.
* Check Caps Lock settings.
* Verify your keyboard layout.

Internet Connection Problems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

A weak or unstable connection may interrupt the login process.

Try these solutions:

* Restart your internet connection.
* Switch to another Wi-Fi network.
* Check mobile data connectivity.
* Temporarily disable VPN or proxy services.

Browser Extension Problems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If the NordPass extension is not working:

#. Restart your browser.
#. Update the extension.
#. Clear browser cache and cookies.
#. Reinstall the extension if needed.

Outdated NordPass Application
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

An outdated app version may cause login or synchronization problems.

Make sure to update:

* NordPass applications.
* Web browsers.
* Operating systems.

Multi-Factor Authentication Problems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If verification fails:

* Enter the correct authentication code.
* Check your authenticator application.
* Confirm your device time settings.
* Use backup verification options if available.

Forgot Your NordPass Master Password?
-------------------------------------

Your Master Password is required to unlock your encrypted password vault.

If you forget it:

* Check your secure password records.
* Use available recovery options.
* Follow NordPass account recovery instructions.

Because password managers use strong encryption, protecting your Master Password is an important part of account security.
